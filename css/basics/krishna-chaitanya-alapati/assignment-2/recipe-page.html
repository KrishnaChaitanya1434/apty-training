Delicious White Sauce Pasta

Ingredients

  • 1 cup (100 grams) pasta (penne or macaroni)
  • One-half cup milk
  • 2 tablespoons butter + half tablespoon butter
  • One-fourth cup heavy cream
  • One-half tablespoon all-purpose flour
  • One-fourth teaspoon black pepper
  • 2 garlic cloves grated
  • One-half teaspoon oregano
  • Salt as needed
  • One-half teaspoon red chili flakes(optional, adjust to taste)

Recipe

  1. Bring 4 cups of water to a rolling boil in a large pot. Add pasta and one-fourth teaspoon salt.
  2. Cook on a medium high flame till it gets to boiling point. Check the instructions on the pasta package for the correct cook time. Check them a minute earlier than mentioned on the package. They must be tender, firm and chewy but not mushy.
  3. Reserve about one-fourt cup pasta cooked stock.
  4. Drain the pasta to a colander. Cover and keep aside.
  5. Pour half tablespoon butter to a pan. Add the vegetables and saute them on a medium high heat until slightly tender yet crunchy. This takes me about 5 to 6 minutes. Remove to a plate and set aside.
  6. Melt 2 tablespoons butter and add minced garlic and fry for 30 to 40 seconds on a medium heat.
  7. Stir in all-purpose flour
  8. Saute well until the flour loses the raw smell, for about a minute. Don't brown the flour. Since this is a small quantity, the roux can become dry and clumpy. It's just fine. Turn off the stove and slowly pour half a cup milk while you stir to incorporate the milk into the roux.
  9. Keep whisking till it looks creamy & uniform to prevent forming lumps. Pour the rest of the milk and stir well.
  10. Turn on the heat and cook on a medium-low until the white sauce becomes thick. Keep stirring and scrape the sides for a uniform sauce through out.
  11. Pour the heavy cream or cheese and mix well.
  12. The white sauce is done perfectly when it is thick enough to coat the back of the spoon.
  13. Add the vegetables and the cooked pasta & mix well.
  14. Add the herbs and spices as per your preference.
